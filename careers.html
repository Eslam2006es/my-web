IT Career Paths

Software Development

Creating applications, systems, and tools for users and organizations. Includes front-end, back-end, full-stack, mobile, and game development.

Average Salary: $110,000

Cybersecurity

Protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks. Roles include ethical hackers, security analysts, and CISOs.

Average Salary: $120,000

Cloud Computing

Designing, implementing, and managing cloud infrastructure using platforms like AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud.

Average Salary: $130,000

Data Science

Extracting insights from complex data sets using statistics, machine learning, and visualization tools.

Average Salary: $125,000

DevOps Engineering

Bridging development and operations to improve collaboration and productivity through automation.

Average Salary: $140,000

IT Management

Leading IT departments, managing projects, and aligning technology with business goals.

Average Salary: $150,000
