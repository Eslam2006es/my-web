Current IT Trends

Remote Work Technologies

The pandemic accelerated adoption of collaboration tools like Zoom, Microsoft Teams, and Slack. Hybrid work models are becoming standard, driving demand for better cybersecurity and cloud solutions.

Edge Computing

Processing data closer to its source reduces latency. Important for IoT devices, autonomous vehicles, and real-time analytics.

AI Ethics and Governance

As AI becomes more powerful, concerns about bias, privacy, and accountability are driving new regulations and ethical frameworks.

Low-Code/No-Code Platforms

Enabling non-programmers to create applications through visual interfaces, democratizing software development.
